# Eventides

Eventides is an event logistics and marketing company. 
            The company offers A-Z event planning services from a team of experienced and energetic event planners,
             suppliers, venues and more. One of the main reasons behind the success of Eventides is the fact that the
              team does not<b> charge a vast fees to its clients!</b> With the number of events we organise, Eventides does not need 
              to add exorbitant fees and mark-ups. This ensures that our clients list, which is 
              constantly growing, make regular use of our services.
